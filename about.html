About AI Evening Classes Oxford

Professional AI training designed for busy Oxford professionals. Small cohorts, face-to-face learning, real results.

Our Mission

We believe AI skills should be accessible to every professional in Oxford, not just technical specialists. Our evening classes provide practical, hands-on training in small cohorts where everyone gets personal attention and real support.

Unlike online courses that leave you isolated or expensive university programs with long wait times, we deliver focused, actionable learning that fits your busy schedule. Face-to-face, one evening per week, with people just like you.

Why We're Different

Small Cohorts, Big Results

We cap every class at 12 students (target 10). This isn't a lecture hall—it's a collaborative workshop where you get individual feedback, ask questions freely, and build real skills through hands-on practice.

Hyper-Local & Face-to-Face

Oxford is home to world-class universities, innovative SMEs, the NHS, and countless professionals seeking to upskill. Our in-person format creates accountability, enables networking, and provides the kind of guided learning that remote courses simply can't match.

Highly Practical

Every session includes hands-on labs and real-world applications. You'll work on mini-projects relevant to your job, get feedback from an experienced instructor, and leave with skills you can use immediately.

Three Clear Pathways

Whether you're a complete beginner, an intermediate user seeking mastery, or a technical professional ready to build AI agents, we have the right course for you. Progress at your own pace through our three-level curriculum.

Our Teaching Philosophy

  • Jobs to be done: We focus on what you want to accomplish—"use AI confidently at work," "automate recurring tasks," "build a data-driven prototype"—not just abstract theory.
  • Safety & ethics first: We teach responsible AI use, bias awareness, and critical evaluation from day one.
  • Immediate applicability: Every technique you learn can be applied to your work the next day.
  • Supportive community: Join a network of Oxford professionals learning AI together, with ongoing support via Discord and office hours.
