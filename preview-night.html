Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any AI experience?

Absolutely not! This preview night is designed for complete beginners. Whether you've never used AI or you're already familiar with ChatGPT, you'll find value in this session.

What's the catch? Why is it free?

No catch! We genuinely want you to experience our teaching style before committing to a full course. We're confident that if you attend, you'll love what we do. But there's no pressure to book a course afterward - many attendees just come for the free training.

Will I be pressured to buy a course?

Not at all. While we'll share information about our full courses and offer an exclusive discount, there's zero pressure. We want you to make the right decision for you, which might be "not right now" - and that's completely fine!

Can I bring a friend or colleague?

Absolutely! Just make sure they register separately as we only have 12 spots total. Learning with a colleague can make the experience even more valuable.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just yourself! While you're welcome to bring a laptop if you'd like to follow along, it's not required. We'll provide everything you need.

What if I can't make it?

No problem! Just let us know as soon as possible so we can offer your spot to someone on the waitlist. Simply email us at the address in your confirmation.

Will this be recorded?

This is an in-person only event designed for live interaction and Q&A. We believe the face-to-face experience is valuable for getting a true sense of our teaching approach.

Where exactly in Oxford is it?

We'll send full venue details including address, parking information, and directions in your confirmation email after you register.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Get in touch and we'll answer any questions you have.
