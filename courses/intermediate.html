What You'll Learn

Go beyond the basics and become truly competent with AI. This intermediate course teaches you professional-grade prompting strategies, multi-step workflows, retrieval techniques, and automation patterns that deliver real productivity gains.

Week 1: Advanced Prompting Tactics

  • Prompt engineering principles for consistent, high-quality outputs
  • Few-shot learning and in-context examples
  • System prompts, temperature, and parameter tuning
  • Debugging poor AI responses
  • Hands-on lab: Craft production-ready prompts

Week 2: Multi-Step Workflows

  • Breaking complex tasks into AI-friendly steps
  • Chain-of-thought and tree-of-thought reasoning
  • Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) basics
  • Using AI with your documents and data
  • Practical applications: research, writing, analysis

Week 3: Automation & Integration

  • Connecting AI to spreadsheets, email, and productivity tools
  • No-code automation (Zapier, Make, n8n)
  • API basics for AI services
  • Building reusable templates and workflows
  • Mini-project: Automate a recurring work task

Week 4: Collaboration & Mastery

  • Team collaboration with AI: etiquette and best practices
  • Quality assurance and output validation
  • Cost management and efficiency tips
  • Capstone: Present your workflow automation
  • Roadmap to agentic AI (preview of Advanced course)
